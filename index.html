About


Anurag Lama, popularly recognised under the alias of "ANXSION" is a polymath hailing from the hills of Darjeeling. A life-long love of nature, science, and mechanics has helped him create a foundation for designs based on an application of functional real-world technical solutions and incorporate many research works for creating exceptional concept arts and product renders.

As a multi-instrumentalist, he makes instrumental compositions, which are progressive, polyrhythmic in nature. He also has a history of film making and has produced background scores for many other videos.

With a particular love for programming, he utilises his experience in digital signal processing to create digital audio processing software as well as other utility softwares. His skill set encompasses of low level embedded system programming to development of advanced websites and software suites.

He is also an lawyer, specialising in civil, criminal and corporate litigation in India.
